How it Works?

Renting a bike via easyride is a very sinple and hassle free process

  1. Go to location where bike stands are available
  2. Select your current location in the rent page and book a bike to get your bike number
  3. Use the bike pin provided in booking details to unlock the bike
  4. When you are ready to return go to any of the nearest bike stands and drop off your bike
  5. Return the bike in the return page and finish your payment
